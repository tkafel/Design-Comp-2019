Overview

WE ARE YOUR FAMILY

We Want You Walk Freely

Many Parkinson's disease patient cannot do multi-tasking, which means they cannot walk and see front at same time. As your family, Easy Stroll want to help you.

No One Help You When You Are In Danger?

We help you! Our product can sends an SOS meesage with your location to your family when we detect your body collision or you press the SOS button.

Have Trouble With Walking?

Our product detects all obstacles in front you. Only thing you need to do is following the laser line on the ground. Our product provides the safest path for you. Your safety is our only concern.

Forget To Report Your Symptoms?

Our product monitor all Parkinsin's disease symptoms you have: freezing, tremor, etc. You can easily find your symptoms data on our app.

using lidar, our product can always detect obstacles in front of you.
our laser module always show the right and safe path for you
with GPS, your family can find you whereever you are
