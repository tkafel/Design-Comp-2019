Easy Stroll

Overview

We Want You

Walk Freely !

Many Parkinson's disease patient cannot do multi-tasking, which means they cannot walk and see front at same time. As your family, Easy Stroll want to help you.
No One Help You When You Are In Danger?
We help you! Our product can sends a SOS message with your location to your family when the product detects your body collision or you press the SOS button.
Have Trouble With Walking?
Our product detects all obstacles in front you. The only thing you need to do is following the laser line on the ground. Our product provides the safest path for you. Your safety is our only concern.
Forget To Report Your Symptoms?
Our product monitors all Parkinsin's disease symptoms you have: freezing, tremor, etc. You can easily find your symptom data on our mobile app.

Technology

Laser Cue
Using 6 line lasers, cue cue cue
Realtime Location
With GPS and GPRS module, your family and caregiver can track where you are historically and in real time in our mobile app
Caregiver Notification
Our system is able to send low latency notification when it detects the walker location freezes, or the user pushed the SOS necklace button
